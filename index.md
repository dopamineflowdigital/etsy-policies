# Privacy Policy — DopamineFlowDigital

Last updated: June 2026

---

## 1. What Data Do We Collect?

When you buy from **DopamineFlowDigital** on Etsy, Etsy collects your information and shares it with me:

- **Your name**
- **Your email address**
- **Your shipping address**
- **Your messages to me** (through Etsy messaging)
- **Your order history** (what you bought, when, how much you paid)

I don't collect any data directly — Etsy does. I only receive what's necessary to deliver your product and help if you have questions.

---

## 2. How Do I Use Your Data?

I use your information **only for:**

✓ Sending you your digital download after purchase  
✓ Answering your support questions  
✓ Updating you if there's an issue with your order  
✓ Following Etsy's requirements for seller operations  

**Marketing emails:** I will **never** send you marketing messages unless you explicitly opt-in and ask for them. No automatic newsletter sign-ups, no hidden email lists.

---

## 3. Who Do I Share Your Data With?

Your data stays between you, me, and Etsy. I share it **only with:**

- **Etsy** (because that's the platform)
- **Payment processors** (credit card companies, PayPal) — handled entirely by Etsy
- **Email service** (if you opt-in to updates) — only if you give permission

I **never** sell, trade, or share your information with anyone else.

---

## 4. How Long Do I Keep Your Data?

- **Etsy keeps** order data for 6 years (their policy)
- **I keep** your information only as long as needed to serve you and meet legal requirements
- After you complete your purchase and I've answered all your questions, I don't hold onto your personal data longer than necessary

If you ask me to delete your data, I will (unless I'm legally required to keep it for tax/order records).

---

## 5. Your Rights

### If you're in the EU (GDPR):

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (ask me what I have about you)
- **Correct** your data (fix wrong information)
- **Delete** your data (in most cases)
- **Object** to how I use it

### If you're in California (CCPA):

You have the right to:
- **Know** what personal information is collected
- **Delete** your personal information (with some exceptions)
- **Opt-out** of selling or sharing your data (I don't do this anyway)

**To exercise these rights, email me at Fitter_L@ukr.net with your request.** I'll respond within 30 days.
